How it works

Features

  • Download music from YouTube, Spotify, and SoundCloud by pasting a link.
  • All downloads are saved as high-quality MP3s with the original title and a unique ID.
  • You find all downloads in your Downloads/music-downloader folder. (MacOs ~/Downloads/music-downloader)
  • Track your downloads and see their status in real time.

Limitations

  • For SoundCloud, only public tracks are supported.
  • For Spotify, you are always rate-limited - it takes longer.
  • Currently on single file downloads are available!
